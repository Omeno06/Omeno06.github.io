ONEPIECE

Friendship and Adventure

Here's a timeline of Onepiece Arc by years

  • 1997 - Romance Dawn Arc and Orangetown Arc
  • 1998 - Syrup Village Arc, Baratie Arc and Arlong Park Arc (Chapter 69).
  • 1999 - Arlong Park Arc (Chapters 70-95), Loguetown Arc, Reverse Mountain Arc, Whisky Peak Arc, Little Garden Arc (Chapters 115-117).
  • 2000 - Little Garden Arc (Chapters 118-129), Drum Island Arc, Arabasta Arc (Chapters 155-165).
  • 2001 - Arabasta Arc (Chapters 166-213).
  • 2002 - Arabasta Arc (Chapters 214-217), Jaya Arc, Skypiea Arc (Chapters 237-259).
  • 2003 - Skypiea Arc (Chapters 260-302), Long Ring Long Land Arc (Chapters 303-304).
  • 2004 - Long Ring Long Land Arc (Chapters 305-321), Water 7 Arc (Chapters 322-349).
  • 2005 - Water 7 Arc (Chapters 350-374), Enies Lobby Arc (Chapters 375-394).
  • 2006 - Enies Lobby Arc (Chapters 395-430), Post-Enies Lobby Arc (Chapters 431-439).
  • 2007 - Post-Enies Lobby Arc (Chapters 440-441), Thriller Bark Arc (Chapters 442-483).
  • 2008 - Thriller Bark Arc (Chapters 484-489), Sabaody Archipelago Arc, Amazon Lily Arc, Impel Down Arc (Chapters 525-526).
  • 2009 - Impel Down Arc (Chapters 527-549), Marineford Arc (Chapters 550-568).
  • 2010 - Marineford Arc (Chapters 569-580), Post-War Arc; Return to Sabaody Arc, Fish-Man Island Arc (Chapters 603-608).
  • 2011 - Fish-Man Island Arc (Chapters 609-649).
  • 2012 - Fish-Man Island Arc (Chapters 650-653), 1st Part of Punk Hazard Arc (Chapters 654-693).
  • 2013 - Punk Hazard Arc (Chapters 694-699), Dressrosa Arc (Chapters 700-732).
  • 2014 - Dressrosa Arc (Chapters 733-771).
  • 2015 - Dressrosa Arc (Chapters 772-801), Zou Arc (Chapters 802-809).
  • 2016 - Zou Arc (Chapters 810-824), Whole Cake Island Arc (Chapters 825-850).
  • 2017 - Whole Cake Island Arc (Chapters 851-889).
  • 2018 - Whole Cake Island Arc (Chapters 890-902), Levely Arc, Wano Country Arc (Chapters 909-928).
  • 2019 - Wano Country Arc (Chapters 929-966).
  • 2020 - Wano Country Arc (Chapters 967-999).
  • 2021 - Wano Country Arc (Chapters 1000-1035).
  • 2022 - Wano Country Arc (Chapters 1036-1057), Egghead Arc (Chapters 1058-1070).
  • 2023 - Egghead Arc (Chapters 1071-current).
